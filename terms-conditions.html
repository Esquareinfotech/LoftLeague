10. Legality

Fantasy Cricket requires its participants to form a team of 11/5 players using their knowledge of the game of cricket, player statistics, players’ performance against a particular opposition/ on a particular ground and players’ performance in a particular format (Test Matches, ODIs and T20). The participants are required to formulate this team within a certain set of guidelines (example- user can only select 3/4/5 batsmen only) and using limited resources (example- total 100 points) and their aim is to maximize the points basis the real game of cricket. Such a selection of a cricket team ensures that the element of skill and knowledge has a predominant influence on the outcome of each contest.

“www.Loft League.com” is a fantasy cricket website/ app that require its participants to form a cricket team as described above and join contests to win real money. Basis the above, the contests on“www. Loft League.com” come under the purview of skill based gaming and are exempt from the provisions of the Public Gambling Act of 1867 since the success of any participant is a function of his/her skill, superior knowledge, judgement and experience about the game of cricket and its players. By participating in the Contests on “www.Loft League.com”, each Participant acknowledges and agrees that he/she is participating in a game of skill.
